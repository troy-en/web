KinitoPET.

About

KinitoPET is a psychological horror game I have been working on! I had the idea a while back sometime in 2019. The Godot game engine was on my radar at the time, and its open source nature really interested me. So over the past few years I have been learning the engine so I could create this vision of KinitoPET. After many smaller attempts, I started serious development on KinitoPET in 2022 with a clear goal and vision.
